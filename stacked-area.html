Percentage of Total Dorm Rooms by Dormitory


We created a stacked area chart to visualize what percentage of the total rooms belonged to each dorm at a given time slice of the lottery. This allows students to compare availability of every dorm for a given year at the same time, and enables students to understand which residence halls will likely remain at a given point in the lottery.

To use this tool, select a year and dorm on the right to highlight it, or click a slice of the chart to find out what dorm it is.
