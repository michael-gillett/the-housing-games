Dorm Comparison


Often, students want to see how quickly rooms are taken in one dorm vs. another. To aid this, we made a visualization that allows students to select two dorms and compare the number of rooms remaining over time for each. This allows students to quickly see the relative popularity of two dorms over time. It also helps to visualize how many rooms each dorm has, relative to the others.
